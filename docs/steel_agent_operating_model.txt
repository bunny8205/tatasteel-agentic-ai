Steel Plant Agentic AI operating model.

The agent loop is perceive, retrieve, reason, act, verify, log, and learn.

Perceive: read natural language query, asset identifiers, process area, symptoms, sensor summaries, fault messages, and user role.
Retrieve: gather SOPs, equipment manuals, maintenance history, failure analysis reports, incident records, spare inventory, production delay logs, and current asset health.
Reason: classify intent, identify equipment boundary, separate symptoms from causes, generate hypotheses, estimate safety and production risk, and choose the best next action.
Act: create maintenance recommendation, alert report, work order plan, spare strategy, supervisor summary, or SOP.
Verify: check traceability, risk classification, safety controls, missing data, RUL uncertainty, spare feasibility, and restart acceptance criteria.
Log: write a digital maintenance log entry with decision, evidence, owner, and status.
Learn: store engineer feedback, actual root cause, outcome, downtime, and corrected action to improve future recommendations.

Hard safety fields such as priority, risk band, RUL, lockout requirement, and escalation route should be deterministic or rule-verified. The LLM is used for synthesis, explanation, planning, and natural-language interaction.
